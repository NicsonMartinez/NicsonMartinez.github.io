About Me:

My relevant qualifications include a Bachelor of Science Degree in Computer Information Systems at the College at Brockport. While studying I also worked as an Information Technology Tools tutor for the Educational Opportunity Program (EOP). In this role, I was responsible for tutoring and helping SUNY Brockport’s incoming college students with Microsoft Office and other college computing applications. Here I refined my communication and my instructional skills that I can use for customer support. During my undergrad experience, I’ve learned core computing concepts, computing languages (such as Java, HTML/CSS, and SQL), troubleshooting hardware and software, and how networking connectives work. Given my skills and qualifications, I believe I would be able to provide technical support for employees, or customers and would be a great addition to your agency. My experience after college in the technical filed, I worked in Hudson Software Corporation as a Technical Support Agent. In this role, I was responsible for diagnosing, troubleshooting, and resolving a high range of hardware and software issues, as well as helping clients of all levels of technical expertise. Today in 2019 I am a student at the Tech Academy where I am preparing myself to become a Junior Full-Stack Software developer. I am currently learning computing languages such as C#, Python, JavaScript, HTML, CSS, SQL and more.
